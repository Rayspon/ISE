About Our School

Our Vision

The International School of Estonia aims to develop inquiring, knowledgeable, and caring young people who help to create a better and more peaceful world through intercultural understanding and respect.

As a small school in central Tallinn, we provide personalized attention to each student, ensuring they develop both academically and personally.

Our Community

Our school is home to students from over 30 nationalities, creating a truly diverse learning environment. This multicultural setting prepares our students to become global citizens who value different perspectives and embrace diversity.

We maintain small class sizes to ensure each student receives individual attention and guidance throughout their educational journey.
